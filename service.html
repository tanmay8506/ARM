The Four Tiers

Choose Your Finish Level

Tier 01

SD — Standard

Traditional, highly precise brush application utilizing premium weightless foundations. Ideal for soft daylight, intimate gatherings, and clients preferring a breathable, radiant, and timelessly clean look.

Tier 02

HD — High Definition

Engineered with light-diffusing microscopically milled pigments that adapt flawlessly to harsh studio lighting and modern DSLR lenses. Completely eliminates camera flashback and blurs skin texture for a smooth, poreless, high-resolution photographic finish.

Tier 03

Airbrush — Veil Finish

Applied via precision micro-compressed air system, creating an impossibly thin, skin-fused barrier. Completely transfer-proof, tear-resistant, and sweat-proof with 12–18 hour endurance — highly recommended for high-humidity settings and multi-day milestones.

Tier 04 — Pinnacle

Luxury — Couture

The pinnacle of bespoke beauty styling. Combines advanced clinical skin prep, hybrid Airbrush-HD dimension sculpting, and exclusive use of ultra-premium artistry houses (Dior, Tom Ford, Chanel). Includes extended continuous touch-up security and a full styling layout for the event.
